Complete a medical certificate of cause of death

Use this digital MCCD to create or complete a Medical certificate of cause of death (MCCD). It is to be used for adults, children and infants dying within the first 28 days of life.

This service is available in Wales as a bilingual service in Welsh and English.

An MCCD is required to register all non-coronial deaths in England and Wales.

This digital MCCD replaces the paper MCCD. It does not replace your current methods of communication or processes between the medical examiner office and attending practitioner. For example, when discussing a cause of death or an amendment to the MCCD you must continue to contact your colleagues by email or telephone.

Who can complete an MCCD

An MCCD can be completed by a doctor who attended the deceased person at any time.

If an attending practitioner cannot be found and the senior coroner decides not to investigate the death, they will refer the case to a medical examiner to certify the death. The medical examiner MCCD will only be used in exceptional circumstances.

Who can use this digital MCCD

If you are an attending practitioner, you can use this digital MCCD to:

  • create an MCCD
  • edit an MCCD

If you are a medical examiner, you can use this digital MCCD to:

  • review an MCCD
  • accept or reject an MCCD

If you are a medical examiner officer, you can use this digital MCCD to:

  • review an MCCD
  • download a completed MCCD to send to the registrar

The local register office will use the completed MCCD to register the death of a deceased person. The family of a deceased person will not view the MCCD.

Before you start

You'll need a secure method of logging in such as your smartcard as supplied by the NHS Care Identity Service.

Before you create or review an MCCD

You'll need the personal details of the deceased person, and medical information relating to the cause of death.

The bilingual Welsh and English MCCD

If you complete the bilingual version of the MCCD you must give your answers in both Welsh and English when requested. You can switch between languages at any time in the journey.

An informant can request a Welsh death certificate from the registrar even if a qualified attending practitioner completes the MCCD in English only.
