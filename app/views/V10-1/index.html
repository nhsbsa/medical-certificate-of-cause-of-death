Complete a medical certificate of cause of death

You can use this service if you are an attending practitioner, a medical examiner or a medical examiner officer.

If you are an attending practitioner you can use this service to:

  • create an MCCD
  • edit an MCCD after scrutiny by a medical examiner

If you are a medical examiner you can use this service to:

  • review an MCCD
  • accept or reject an MCCD

If you are a medical examiner officer you can use this service to:

  • review an MCCD
  • download a completed MCCD to send to the registrar

The local register office will use the completed MCCD to register the death of a deceased person. The family of a deceased person will not view the MCCD.

The bilingual Welsh and English MCCD service

As you complete the bilingual version of the MCCD service you must give your answers in both English and Welsh when requested.

An informant can request a Welsh death certificate from the registrar even if a qualified attending practitioner completes the MCCD in English only. The registrar will generate a Welsh death certificate automatically from the English content of the MCCD. Your local register office will not use the Welsh content of the MCCD.
