You need to complete the paper MCCD for neonatal deaths (Form 65)

You cannot create a Medical certificate of cause of death (MCCD) for a person who died during the first 28 days of life yet. This is because the online service is still being developed and not everyone can use it yet.

You can still complete the paper neonatal MCCD (Form 65)

You can get a Form 65 MCCD booklet from:

  • the administration team if you work in a community setting
  • the bereavement team if you work in a trust
