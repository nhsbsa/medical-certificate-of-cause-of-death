Create a Medical certificate of cause of death (MCCD)

One of the main benefits of this work is to improve what is a difficult experience for the bereaved by removing the need for the bereaved to transport the MCCD in a sealed envelope to the registrar in person and instead implementing a digital transfer of the MCCD.

Iterations

Version 11 - Post Alpha experiments

Post-Alpha experiments

Design and testing of prioritised features from the Beta Roadmap

What we created

  • Additional questions for the ME cert added
  • First iteration of Office dashboard created
  • Statuses of the MCCD moving through the process
  • Translations loaded from spreadsheet for bilingual path
  • MCCD output for registration

What we plan to test

  • ME Cert - Medical examiners completing an MCCD
  • MEO use of the service
  • Bilingual MCCD
  • PDF formats to be sent to the Local Register Office for registration

What we found

  • The additional questions for ME Certs caused no issues
  • Many users are still not familiar with CIS2
  • One office dashboard can be used by MEs and MEOs with actions specific to their roles
  • It will be valuable to ME and MEO users to search, sort or filter the dashboard to help find cases
  • A toggle for English and Welsh is expected on the Bilingual service
  • Users are somewhat frustrated by questions with multiple inputs to support dual entry in Welsh and English, however the majority understand why they are there

Any known issues

  • Mapping of AP organisations to ME organisations
  • Rolling out in Wales while CIS2 is not supported
  • Users expect more integration to reduce the cognitive load on them
  • GDS design patterns are not best adapted for internal services such as dashboards and case management, so the table on dashboard is not optimal if users chose to access on mobile
